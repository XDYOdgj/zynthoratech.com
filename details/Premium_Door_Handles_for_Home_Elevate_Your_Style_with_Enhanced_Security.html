Probrico Gold Privacy Door Handles

Upgrade your home or office with Probrico's Gold Privacy Door Handles, where style meets strength. Crafted from durable zinc alloy, these door knobs are engineered for long-term use and provide exceptional resistance to rust, rain, and fog, making them ideal for any environment.

Featuring a heavy-duty lock system with ANSI Grade 3 certification, these handles ensure top-tier security for your doors. Whether you're using them daily or in emergency situations, you can trust them to perform reliably. The adjustable latch design accommodates most standard doors, allowing for a seamless fit and easy customization.

Plus, the interchangeable left/right settings make installation simple, empowering you to choose the perfect fit for your space.
