Square Deadbolt Lock with Matte Black Finish

The TooCust Square Single Cylinder Deadbolt is built for both residential and commercial use, offering a perfect blend of security and style. Its thickened anti-theft solid latch is engineered to provide reliable protection against forced entry, ensuring peace of mind for your home or business.

Constructed from high-quality materials, this deadbolt meets ANSI/BHMA Grade 3 standards for strength and durability. The matte black finish not only gives a modern look but also resists rust, guaranteeing long-term use without fading or corrosion.

Key Features

  • High security with thickened anti-theft solid latch
  • ANSI/BHMA Grade 3 deadbolt for durability and strength
  • Anti-rust matte black finish for long-lasting use
  • Adjustable latch fits door thicknesses from 1-3/8" to 1-3/4"

Why Choose This Deadbolt?

  • Easy to install with included Phillips head screwdriver
  • Keyed 1-side design for simple exterior locking
  • Turn button for effortless interior access
  • Fully reversible for left and right-handed doors

Whether securing your front door or an exterior entrance, this single cylinder deadbolt delivers the reliability you need, combined with a sleek matte black design that complements any style.
