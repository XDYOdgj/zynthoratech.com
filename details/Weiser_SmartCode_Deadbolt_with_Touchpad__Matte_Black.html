Weiser SmartCode Deadbolt

Upgrade your door security with a sleek, modern, and reliable electronic deadbolt that combines style with smart technology.

Smart Access Anywhere

The Weiser SmartCode Deadbolt features an intuitive electronic touchpad that allows you to unlock your door with a personalized code. Whether you're home or away, this keyless entry solution offers convenience and peace of mind. With bright LED illumination, you'll never have trouble entering your code, even in low light.

Secure and Easy to Use

Designed for both residential and commercial use, this deadbolt provides a secure and stylish upgrade over traditional locks. The system stores up to 50 user codes with a master code option, making it easy to manage access for family, guests, or roommates. Plus, its fingerprint-resistant surface adds an extra layer of privacy and protection.

Key Features

  • Electronic touchpad for secure, code-based entry
  • Fast and quiet motorized operation
  • Stores up to 50 user codes with master code access
  • Includes SmartKey technology for hassle-free re-keying
  • Bright LED lighting for clear code visibility
  • Low battery alerts and simple maintenance for long-term use

Why Choose This Deadbolt?

Whether you're looking for a modern touchpad deadbolt, a secure deadbolt with keyless entry, or a durable matte black deadbolt, the Weiser SmartCode Deadbolt is built for performance and convenience. It offers a seamless blend of security, style, and technology, designed to enhance your daily life and protect your home with ease.
